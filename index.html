MAILIL WOMEN GROUP/PROJECT

The MAILIL women's group/project is an inspiring initiative launched by the women of Turkana East in 2018 to provide support to the vulnerable community in the sub-county. The region, characterized by an arid climate, faces challenges in agricultural productivity and access to water resources, leading to a situation where many residents live below the poverty line and lack access to basic necessities. Women encounter significant obstacles in terms of education, employment, and decision-making within this context.

VISSION

To empower Turkana East women through sustainable livelihood initiatives, fostering economic independence and community development.

MISSION

To provide Turkana East women with the skills, resources, and opportunities to improve their livelihoods, enhance their quality of life, and contribute positively to their communities.

OBJECTIVES

Economic Empowerment:

Promote income-generating activities, such as agriculture, livestock rearing, and small-scale businesses.

Provide access to financial services, including loans and savings.

Implement vocational training programs to equip women with marketable skills.

Capacity Building:

Foster leadership and decision-making skills among women.

Enhance women's knowledge and understanding of their rights and responsibilities.

Promote gender equality and women's empowerment within the community.

Community Development:

Improve access to essential services, including healthcare, education, and infrastructure.

Strengthen community governance and participation.

Promote sustainable resource management and environmental conservation.

Partnership and Collaboration:

Establish partnerships with government agencies, NGOs, and the private sector to leverage resources and expertise.

Collaborate with other women's groups and organizations to share knowledge and best practices.

Monitoring and Evaluation:

Implement a robust monitoring and evaluation system to track progress and identify areas for improvement.

Use data to inform decision-making and ensure accountability.
